Our Story

A Family Dream Come True

Kevin's Kones started as a dream shared around our family dinner table. In 2018, after years of talking about opening our own ice cream shop, we finally took the leap. Those first few months were anything but easy. We poured every penny we had into this little shop on Arrow Ave, working long hours, learning hard lessons, and wondering more than once if we'd made the right choice. There were nights we stayed up late perfecting recipes and mornings we opened the doors not knowing if anyone would walk in.

But slowly, things changed. Word spread about our homemade flavors and the welcoming atmosphere we worked so hard to create. Neighbors became regulars, and regulars became friends. Now, years later, we can honestly say we have no regrets. Watching families create memories over scoops of ice cream, seeing kids light up when they try our Superhero flavor for the first time, and being a part of this wonderful Winter Park community has made every challenge worth it. This shop isn't just our business; it's our heart.

Winter Park, 1987

What We're All About

Kevin's Kones is a family-owned ice cream shop in Winter Park, Florida, serving up happiness one scoop at a time. From creamy soft-serve and hand-scooped classics to refreshing slushies, thick milkshakes, and old-fashioned floats, we have something for everyone. We believe ice cream is more than just a treat; it's a reason to slow down, gather with the people you love, and savor the sweet moments in life.
